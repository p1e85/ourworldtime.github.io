Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 21, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Our World Time. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have.

1. Data We Collect

We collect two types of information:

A. Functional Data Stored in Your Browser

To make the website work correctly, we store some information directly in your browser's Local Storage. This data is not sent to us or any server; it stays on your computer. This includes:

  • Pro Status (isPro): A simple flag (true/false) to remember if you have unlocked the Pro Version.
  • Backup Code (unlockCode): The unique code provided after a donation, allowing you to restore Pro access.
  • View Preference (currentView): For Pro users, we remember whether you prefer the Free or Pro layout.
  • Favorite Timezone: If you set a favorite timezone, we save it so it's there when you return.

B. Anonymous Usage Data

To understand how our visitors use the website and to identify areas for improvement, we use a third-party analytics service called Microsoft Clarity. This service helps us by collecting anonymous data about your interactions, such as:

  • Clicks, scrolls, and mouse movements (heatmaps).
  • Anonymous recordings of your session on the site.
  • General information about your device, operating system, and browser.

2. How We Use Your Data

The functional data stored in your browser is used only to provide you with the features of the website, such as enabling the Pro Version. We do not have access to this information.

The anonymous usage data collected by Microsoft Clarity is used solely for the purpose of improving the website's design, usability, and performance.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Microsoft Clarity for analytics. By using our website, you understand that your anonymous interaction data is being processed by Microsoft. You can review their privacy practices by reading the Microsoft Privacy Statement.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at p1creationsco@gmail.com.
